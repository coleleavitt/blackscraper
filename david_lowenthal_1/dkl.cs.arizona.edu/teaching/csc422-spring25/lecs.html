Lectures

Attendance at lectures is optional. You are, however, responsible for all material covered in lectures. All lecture materials as well as examples will be posted to the class D2L. I will do my best to make lectures interesting. I believe that you won't just be able to read the notes and succeed. You should come to lecture prepared. This means you have read the assigned material in advance.

Lectures will not be recorded.

During lecture, please do not hesitate to ask questions; you might think your question is silly, but more likely, many people are wondering about the same thing.
