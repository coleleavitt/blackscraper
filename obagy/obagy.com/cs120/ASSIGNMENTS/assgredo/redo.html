CSc 120 (Spring 2025): Assignment Redo Opportunity

Submission Deadline: 7PM Mon May 5

You are being given the chance to rewrite a previous long assignment in order to replace your current score on that assignment with a better score. Your score for the assignment will only be replaced if it is greater than your current score. Your score can only improve!

Guidelines

  1. You can redo any long assignment of your choice. (That is, you can only redo the long portion of the assignment, not the short.)
  2. If there are two programs in the long assignment, you must submit them both.
  3. You must follow the restrictions given for the original assignment.
  4. There is no Late Day submission for this redo opportunity. (The TAs need time to grade it before the Reading Day.)
  5. Once your submission has been graded, you will receive the higher score between your original submission and the new submission.

How to submit

You must have the following TWO submissions to Gradescope by the deadline:

  1. For the assignment you choose, you will submit your assignment to the FOR-LATE-SUBMISSIONS folder for that assignment. This will run the autograder for that assignment so that you can test your submission as usual.

  2. In the folder Assignment-Redo-Opportunity in Gradescope, submit a file called redo.txt that has your name and the assignment number. Here is an example of a redo.txt file:
    Name: Student Smith
    Assignment: 8
