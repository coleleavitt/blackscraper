Stony Brook RTG in Geometry

Department of Mathematics and Simons Center for Geometry and Physics

Overview

The joint Math / SCGP training program in geometry is an NSF-funded project intended to strengthen the training of students and postdocs in geometry (broadly interpreted). The philosophy is to combine the advantages of a traditional mathematics department with the unique opportunities of an international research institute located next door.

Besides providing funding for eligible participants, the RTG sponsors a number of activities that should be of interest to many students and postdocs. These include:

  • A professional development seminar for advanced students and postdocs to help participants “learn the ropes” of the mathematics profession.
  • A seminar aimed at helping beginning graduate students navigate the first years of graduate school.
  • In addition, the RTG supports activities aimed at undergraduates both at Stony Brook and in the greater NYC region.

We are also very open to suggestions for other activities that the RTG might sponsor.
